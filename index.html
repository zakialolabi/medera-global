About Medera Global

Care coordination for international patients in Georgia.

Medera Global brings international patients together with outstanding surgeons from around the world. We bring top doctors to perform surgeries and provide care in Tbilisi. Patients from countries with limited access to advanced procedures can finally receive the treatments they need.

We work only with the best hospitals and private clinics in Tbilisi, selected for their medical standards, safety, and reliability. Each case is matched to the right setting and specialists, depending on the medical needs.

Our team coordinates the entire process. We collect and translate medical documents, schedule consultations, align treatment plans, and provide local support when needed. From your first question to full recovery, we stay by your side.

Communication is available in English, Hebrew, Russian, and Georgian.
Our goal is clear. To make world class care possible, accessible, and smooth for every patient who needs it.

Our mission

We believe that access to excellent healthcare should not depend on where you live. Medera Global brings together top surgeons and the best clinics in Tbilisi to make high-quality treatment available to patients from around the world.

Beyond individual journeys, we are building a system that opens long-term access to care, so more people can get the treatment they need, when they need it.

We focus on simplicity, safety, and support, so your energy goes into healing.
