About Medera Global

Care coordination for international patients in Georgia.

Medera Global is a Tbilisi based care coordination group that connects international patients with world class doctors at accredited hospitals and private clinics in Georgia. We turn a complex journey into a simple plan that begins with your first question and ends with your return home.

Your coordinator is a single point of contact for the entire pathway. We collect and translate medical records, schedule consultations, align specialists, and organise travel support when needed. During treatment we are present and responsive, so you never feel alone or uncertain about next steps.

Our partner network is chosen for clinical outcomes, safety, and clear communication. We work with teams that explain options plainly, respect your time, and focus on results that matter to you. Every case receives a tailored plan that reflects your health goals, budget, and timing.

Trust is built through clarity. You always know who is treating you, what is planned, and how decisions are made. Confidentiality is standard. Communication is available in English, German, Hebrew, and Russian. The goal is simple. Better care, fewer barriers, and a calm experience from consultation to full recovery.
